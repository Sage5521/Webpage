About Me:

Hello! My name is Sage Howard, and this is a portfolio of my work. I am currently a student at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill studying Sociology and Studio Art. This site will have work relating to my studies in sociology as well as my work in studio art. In addition to this I am currently learning HTML,CSS, and JavaScript in my free time, in fact this is the first true webpage I am making that will hopefully have javascript functionality.

About My Art:

I do watercolor paintings, that usually focus on plowers, plants, or natural scenery. In addition to my work with watercolors I also work with clay making ceramics. I usually prefer to throw on the wheel, but I do also do some hand built work. I also have some digital illustrations, but most of them are from quite a while ago since I don't typically do them unless I have a reason or want to make somethings that I can't make physically. I'm sure that while at school I wil dabble in more art forms, so I will make sure to keep this updated with whatever I am working on.
